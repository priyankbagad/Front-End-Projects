Coronavirus updates: India cases at 21,797; death toll reaches 681
The Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR) recorded 21,797 coronavirus (Covid-19) cases in India by Thursday, and the death toll stood at 681. A total of 500,542 samples from 485,172 individuals havde been tested as on April 23, said ICMR. Globally, 2,646,452 people have so far been infected, of whom 184,353 have died, according to Worldometer. At 5,652, Maharashtra has the highest number of cases among Indian states. It is followed by Gujarat (2,407), Delhi (2,248), Rajasthan (1,935), Madhya Pradesh (1,592), Tamil Nadu (1,629), and Uttar Pradesh (1,449). The head of the World Health Organization (WHO) earlier said there were "worrying upward trends" in early epidemics in parts of Africa and central and South America, warning that the "virus would be with us for a long time".
